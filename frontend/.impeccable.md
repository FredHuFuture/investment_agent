# Grip — Design Context

## Target Audience
Retail investors who regularly check the app to monitor their portfolio, analyze tickers, and track signals. They want to quickly scan key numbers, see what changed, and act on insights.

## Use Cases
- Check portfolio value and P&L at a glance
- Analyze individual tickers with AI agent signals
- Monitor watchlist for price targets and signals
- Review backtest results and signal accuracy
- Track risk metrics and alerts

## Brand Personality
**Modern Craft** — Like a beautifully designed financial newspaper. Editorial, typographic, intelligent.
- Warm, not cold. Confident, not flashy.
- Information-dense but never cluttered.
- Typography-driven hierarchy, not decoration-driven.
- The interface should feel like it was designed by a human with taste, not generated by AI.

## Key Memorable Quality
Easy to pick up, immediately usable (容易上手，直接就能用). No learning curve. Important info front and center.

## Design System
- **Fonts**: Source Serif 4 (display/headings), Plus Jakarta Sans (body/UI), JetBrains Mono (data/numbers)
- **Colors**: Warm gray palette (CSS variables), emerald green accent, semantic up/down/caution
- **Theme**: Supports light and dark mode via CSS custom properties on the gray scale
- **Cards**: Solid backgrounds with subtle shadows, no glassmorphism
- **Layout**: Editorial — varied typography creates hierarchy, not identical card grids
- **Motion**: Staggered fade-up on entry, smooth transitions, purposeful
